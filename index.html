Who We Are

We're Logan and Ashlin — a local firefighter and teacher who have called Linn & Jones County home our entire lives. Between the two of us, we've done more than our fair share of moving around the area, and we know firsthand how stressful it can be. We started Totes2Move because we wanted to take at least one thing off your plate — no hunting for boxes, no tape, no hauling cardboard to the recycling bin after. Just sturdy totes delivered to your door and picked up when you're done. Moving is hard enough. The boxes shouldn't be.
